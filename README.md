# Artificial Intelligence and Digital Footprints

## Overview
This project explores the relationship between Artificial Intelligence, digital footprints, and online privacy. It aims to raise awareness about how personal data is collected, analyzed, and used, while highlighting cybersecurity risks such as data breaches, identity theft, and online fraud.

## Project Highlights
- Artificial Intelligence and Privacy
- Digital Footprints
- Digital Identity
- Data Breaches
- Identity Theft
- Online Safety Awareness
- Educational Booth
- Interactive Activities
- Awareness Flyer
